Macronutrients and micronutrients are essential for optimal health. Micronutrients, such as vitamins and minerals, play vital roles in maintaining cellular function, immune system health, and overall well-being.

Engaging in regular exercise offers numerous benefits for both physical and mental well-being. Furthermore, regular physical activity is linked to enhanced mood, reduced stress, and improved cognitive function.
